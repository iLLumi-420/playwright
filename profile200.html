About

As an adjunct professor, I teach undergraduate students Business Intelligence (BI) and Business Strategies. Students learn that BI is a set of processes, technologies, and tools that convert raw data into meaningful and valuable information for business analysis and decision-making. In Business Strategies, students explore business and corporate-level strategies, competitive rivalry, acquisitions and restructuring, alliances, corporate governance, international strategy, and entrepreneurship.
